## 🏢 Concept Fabora Impact - Partie RH

**Fabora Impact** est un cabinet RH innovant basé en Afrique de l'Ouest (Sénégal, Bénin, Niger, Burkina Faso, Togo) spécialisé dans :

- 🎯 **Recrutement** de talents qualifiés
- 👥 **Intérim** et mise à disposition de personnel
- 📊 **Plateforme digitale RH** sécurisée pour la conformité et les audits
- 🎓 **Formation et Coaching** individuel et collectif
- 📝 **Conseil RH** et élaboration de politiques RH

### Vision

> _« Des pratiques RH alignées et conformes pour un impact réel »_

### Valeurs clés

- **Professionnalisme** - **Transparence** - **Conformité** - **Redevabilité** - **Impact**

## ✨ Prompt Stitch pour Hero Section RH

### Prompt à copier pour Stitch :

```
Create a modern, professional hero section for "Fabora Impact" - an African HR consulting firm.

VISUAL STYLE:
- Clean, corporate yet warm design
- African-inspired subtle geometric patterns in background
- Gradient from deep emerald green (#1E6B3C) to soft mint (#C8E6D5)
- Accent touches of vibrant orange (#E8622A)
- Professional photography feel with diverse African professionals

MAIN ELEMENTS:
- Large headline: "Des talents qualifiés pour un impact réel"
- Subheadline: "Recrutement • Intérim • Conformité RH • Formation"
- Primary CTA button in orange (#E8622A): "Découvrir nos services"
- Secondary CTA in outlined green: "Nous contacter"

IMAGERY:
- Diverse African business professionals collaborating
- Modern office environment with digital screens
- Symbolism of growth: subtle plant/tree elements
- Connected dots/network pattern representing talent network

MOOD:
- Professional and trustworthy
- Dynamic and forward-thinking
- Warm and human-centered
- African pride with international standards

LAYOUT:
- 60/40 split: text left, visual right
- Floating cards showing services icons
- Subtle animation suggestion: growing tree, connecting dots
- White space for breathing room

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Raleway ExtraBold for headline
- Clean sans-serif for body
- Strong visual hierarchy

COLOR DISTRIBUTION:
- Primary background: soft ivory (#F9F9F7)
- Green as dominant brand color (60%)
- Orange for CTAs and highlights (10%)
- Neutral grays for text (#1C1C1A, #5A5A56)
```
